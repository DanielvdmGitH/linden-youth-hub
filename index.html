About the Initiating Community

Vida Church Linden

The Linden Youth Hub was born from a vision within the Vida Church community — a Gospel-centred family rooted in Linden, Johannesburg. Vida's heartbeat, drawn from Zechariah 11:16, is to "Care for the lost, Seek the Young, Heal the injured, Feed the Healthy." The Youth Hub is a direct expression of that mission — serving the broader community of Linden, not any single congregation.

Vida was founded in 2022 by Pastor Hendrik Van Deventer and his family, beginning with small gatherings in a living room in Linden. Today it gathers weekly at Pinnacle College, Linden. The name Vida means life in Latin — and life, in all its fullness, is what the Youth Hub is about.

🌐 Visit vidachurch.co.za 📘 Facebook 📸 Instagram @vidachurchsa ▶ YouTube @VidaChurchSA ✉ vidachurchsa@gmail.com

⚠ Important note for donors & businesses

The Linden Youth Hub is an independent community initiative. Donations to the Building Fund are used exclusively for the construction phases of the Youth Hub. Funds are not used for church operations, staff salaries, or any Vida Church expenses. Phase 1 contributions build the pickleball court — nothing else.
